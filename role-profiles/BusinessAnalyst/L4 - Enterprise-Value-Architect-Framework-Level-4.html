Pro Tip: The DECISION Framework

When designing any enterprise decision framework, always use the DECISION framework:

  • Decision Type: What category of decision is this?
  • Enterprise Impact: How does this affect the broader organization?
  • Criteria: What specific metrics will determine the right choice?
  • Influencers: Who needs to be involved in the decision process?
  • Scope: What constraints define the boundaries of this decision?
  • Implementation: How will the decision be executed and monitored?
  • Ownership: Who has final decision authority?
  • Next Steps: What follow-up is needed after the decision is made?

This transforms "I need to make a decision" into "Here's how we make this type of decision consistently"—making you an enterprise value architect rather than just a business analyst.
