Pro Tip: The Quality Vision Framework

When defining quality strategy, always use the V.I.S.I.O.N. framework:

  • Value first: "What business value does this quality approach create?"
  • Integration: "How does this integrate across the organizational ecosystem?"
  • Strategic alignment: "How does this align with business strategy and market trends?"
  • Innovation potential: "What innovation opportunities does this enable?"
  • Organizational readiness: "How prepared is the organization to adopt this approach?"
  • Next evolution: "How will this approach evolve with changing business needs?"

This transforms "How should we test this?" into "How can quality create competitive advantage in this market?"—making you a true quality visionary rather than just a quality strategist.
