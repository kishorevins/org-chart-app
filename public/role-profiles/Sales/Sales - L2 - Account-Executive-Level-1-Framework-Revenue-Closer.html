Productivity Killers & Fixes

Based on 2024 AE Performance Research – These are proven time-wasters that sabotage deal velocity.

Killer Impact Simple Fix
Chasing Unqualified Deals AEs waste 40%+ time on deals with low close probability (Gartner 2024) → "Deal Scorecard" System:
- Score each deal weekly on budget, authority, need, timeline
- Use color-coded pipeline view (Green/Yellow/Red)
- Weekly review with manager to prune low-probability deals
Manual Proposal Creation AEs spend 15+ hours weekly on proposal formatting (Salesforce Research) → "Proposal Template Stack":
- Create 5-7 scenario-based templates
- Use AI tools for first draft generation
- Implement version control with clear naming convention
Poor Handoff to CSM 28% of customers experience onboarding delays due to poor handoffs (TSIA) → "Customer Context Map":
- Document 3 key customer personas with motivations
- Outline top 3 customer expectations
- Schedule joint kickoff with CSM before deal close
