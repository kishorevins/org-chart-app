Critical "Don'ts" for Revenue Engine Architects

  • 🚫 Never treat all reps the same without personalized development paths
    (Wastes potential of high performers and fails to address specific skill gaps)
  • 🚫 Never prioritize activity metrics over outcome metrics
    (Creates busy work without revenue impact and misaligns team priorities)
  • 🚫 Never allow CRM to become a compliance exercise rather than a performance tool
    (Results in inaccurate data, poor forecasting, and missed coaching opportunities)
