Vice President - Performance, Accountability & Service Framework

The Business Owner: Defining where the company goes, where it plays, how it grows, and how leadership strength turns strategy into scale.
Business Ownership, P&L & Growth Direction
Organization Point of View
Overarching Goal: To shape the future of the company by owning strategic direction, growth choices, investments, leadership quality, and the overall business engine required for scale.
Growth Killer to Avoid: Operating like a senior manager instead of a business owner. This role fails when it gets trapped in daily operations and does not make sharp choices on markets, investments, leadership quality, and growth bets.

Success Signal

The company knows where it is going, invests with discipline, grows with profit quality, and has a leadership bench that can carry the next phase.

Failure Signal

Too many priorities, unclear bets, reactive investment, growth without control, and overdependence on a few strong individuals.
